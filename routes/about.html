ABOUT GALAXOFIT

Located just outside of Settlement 008 in Planet BX31D9, GalaxoFit is the largest indoor sports complex in the New System, providing regulation courts and playing fields for more than a dozen sports under one roof. This is the only sports complex in the New System where you can experience earth like gravity which we call GalaxoEarth for a few selected sports.In addition to the 700,000-square-foot indoor facility, The GalaxoFit offers an outdoor field hockey pitch and a climate controlled dome used for field hockey, soccer, football, and more. The GalaxoFit is the premier destination for clubs, tournaments and competitions. Make it an overnight experience and stay at the GalxoLife Resort located within GalaxoFit.

But that’s just the beginning. The GalaxoFit also features extensive recreational facilities, including a climbing center, fitness center, food court and arcade, as well as event and meeting spaces. It’s a place where athletes, fans, families, and businesses can get together in groups large and small to enjoy unique, world-class facilities.

From individual fitness training to national sporting events, The GalaxoFit delivers an unparalleled experience for all of our guests.

THE GALAXOEARTH SYSTEM

Do you want your basketball to bounce like it used to bounce on earth? Do you want your sports experience to be just like the old times? We got you covered. Now you can experience a pull like that of earth in our all new GalaxoEarth Sports. Designed by SPACESENSE this system is based on the fundamentals of Artificial Gravity. This system changes the gravity from 9.0m/s2 to 9.8m/s2. The GalaxoEarth system has been tested for a whole year and in all conditions therby assuring the safety.
